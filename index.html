NYC Subway Ridership
This is an interactive web map of the New York City subway system. The data displayed shows average weekday ridership numbers across the entire city from 2015-2020. 2020 saw a massive dip in ridership as the COVID-19 pandemic kept many commuters at home throughout the year. Times Square/42nd street, NYC’s busiest station, saw a 68.7% decrease in ridership as a result. In 2019 there was an average of 205,159 riders per weekday, but in 2020 the average fell to 64,301. Grand Central Station/42nd Street, NYC’s second busiest station saw a similar pattern, decreasing 71.5% from 2019 to 2020. In 2019 there was an average of 157,273 riders per weekday, and in 2020 the average fell to 44,778.
